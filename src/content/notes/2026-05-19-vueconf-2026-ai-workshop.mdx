Co-led an AI workshop with [Martin Rojas](https://www.martinrojas.dev/) at **VueConf US 2026** at Georgia Tech this past Tuesday. The room was small and curious. Most attendees came from defense and government, and most weren't asking *can AI write my Vue components*. They were asking the harder question: *how do I raise the AI IQ of my org without breaking what already works?*

That reframed the workshop for me. The frontier for these folks is organizational. Procurement, evaluation, the gap between a developer who's been using these tools for a year and a director who has to authorize them. The technical bar to use AI keeps falling, while the institutional bar to adopt it inside a regulated org is where the real work is.

Fun to be on the receiving end of that question for a change.
